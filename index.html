What is InteractMaths?

InteractMaths is the name of a 3rd year Natural Sciences project, at the University of Exeter created with the help of students and staff from Exeter Mathematics School.
The goal of the project is to create a resource that Year 12‑equivalent students can use to learn about the mathematics of Matrices!


On this website you will find several types of resources:

  • Prezi presentations provide an introduction and overview of a topic, as well as helping to explain the terminology and concepts you will meet as you delve deeper into this branch of mathematics.
  • Worksheets with answers available will allow you to develop and test your understanding through practice.
  • Finally Videos of worked problems provide another way to learn, or explain the process of solving these problems.
  • We also have a Glossary, and bibliography containing links to other resources you may wish to use in your time studying matricies.
