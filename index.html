What is InteractMaths?

Well, InteractMaths is the name of a 3rd year Natural Sciences project, created with the help of students and staff from Exeter Mathematics School. The goal of the project is to create a resource that Year 12‑equivalent students and teachers can use to learn about the the mathematics of Matricies!
